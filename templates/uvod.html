Ask Us Anything

Common questions about our repair service

How long does it take?

Most repairs are completed within 3-5 business days from when we receive your device. More complex repairs might take longer, but we'll always keep you updated on the progress.

What if my device can't be fixed?

If our technicians determine that your device cannot be repaired, we'll return it to you and provide a detailed explanation. You'll only be charged a diagnostic fee in this case, which will be waived if you choose to proceed with the repair.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, we offer a warranty on all repairs. The warranty duration and coverage depend on the type of repair and parts used. Please ask your repair specialist for details.

How do I track my repair?

Once your repair is underway, we'll provide you with a tracking number and a link to monitor the status of your repair online.
